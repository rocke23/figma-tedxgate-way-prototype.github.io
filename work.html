What is TED?

TED is a nonprofit devoted to Ideas to Ideas Worth Spreading. It started out (in 1984) as a conference bringing together people from three worlds: Technology, Enterainment, Design. Since then then its scope has become ever broader.

TEDxGateway?

TEDxGateway is an independently organised TED event operated under license from TED. At the day long conference we will feature eminent speakers from different walks of life will share unique ideas from India and all around the globe.
